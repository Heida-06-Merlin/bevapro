Oreo Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 5-6 Oreo cookies
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Oreo cookies for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, crush the Oreo cookies into small pieces.
  • Add milk and vanilla ice cream to the blender.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the Oreo milkshake into glasses.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with additional Oreo cookies.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Oreo Milkshake!
