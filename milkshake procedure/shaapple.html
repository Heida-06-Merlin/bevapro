Apple Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 apples, peeled, cored, and diced
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Peel, core, and dice the apples.
  • In a blender, combine the diced apples, milk, honey or sugar, and ground cinnamon.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the shake and adjust sweetness or cinnamon according to your preference.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the apple shake into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Apple Shake!
