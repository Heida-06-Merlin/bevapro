Bourbon Shake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup bourbon
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup or honey
  • 1-2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the bourbon, milk, maple syrup or honey, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the bourbon shake into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Bourbon Shake!
