Butterscotch Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups vanilla ice cream
  • 1/2 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 3 tablespoons butterscotch syrup
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Butterscotch chips for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the vanilla ice cream, milk, and butterscotch syrup.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If the shake is too thick, you can add more milk to achieve the desired consistency.
  • Pour the butterscotch shake into glasses.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with butterscotch chips.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Butterscotch Shake!
