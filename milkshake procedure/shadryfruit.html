Dry Fruit Shake

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup mixed dry fruits (almonds, cashews, pistachios, raisins, etc.)
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1-2 scoops vanilla ice cream (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • If the dry fruits are not already chopped, finely chop them into small pieces.
  • In a blender, combine the chopped dry fruits, milk, honey or sugar, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the dry fruit shake into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the nutritious Dry Fruit Shake!
