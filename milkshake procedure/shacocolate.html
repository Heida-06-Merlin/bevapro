Chocolate Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 4 tablespoons chocolate syrup or cocoa powder
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or sprinkles for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the milk, chocolate syrup or cocoa powder, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the chocolate shake into glasses.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or sprinkles.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the indulgent Chocolate Shake!
