Strawberry Shake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and chopped
  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1-2 scoops vanilla ice cream (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the chopped strawberries, milk, honey or sugar, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the strawberry shake into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Strawberry Shake!
