Muskmelon Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups muskmelon (cantaloupe), peeled, seeded, and diced
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Peel, seed, and dice the muskmelon.
  • In a blender, combine the diced muskmelon, milk, and honey or sugar.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • If the shake is too thick, you can add more milk to achieve the desired consistency.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the muskmelon shake into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Muskmelon Shake!
