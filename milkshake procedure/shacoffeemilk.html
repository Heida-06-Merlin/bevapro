Coffee Milkshake

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 1-2 tablespoons instant coffee powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar or sweetener, adjust to taste
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Ice cubes (optional)
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the milk, instant coffee powder, sugar, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • If desired, add ice cubes to the blender and blend again for a colder shake.
  • Pour the coffee milkshake into glasses.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or cocoa powder.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Coffee Milkshake!
