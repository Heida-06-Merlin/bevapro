Nutella Shake

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • 4 tablespoons Nutella
  • 2 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • Whipped cream for topping (optional)
  • Chopped hazelnuts or chocolate shavings for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a blender, combine the milk, Nutella, and vanilla ice cream.
  • Blend until smooth and creamy.
  • Pour the Nutella shake into glasses.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with chopped hazelnuts or chocolate shavings.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the delicious Nutella Shake!
