Masala Chai

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 tablespoons loose black tea leaves or tea bags
  • 2-3 whole green cardamom pods, lightly crushed
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 4-5 whole cloves
  • 1 small piece of fresh ginger, thinly sliced
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or sweetener (adjust to taste)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add black tea leaves or tea bags, crushed cardamom pods, cinnamon stick, cloves, and sliced ginger to the boiling water.
  • Allow the spices to simmer in the water for about 5 minutes to infuse their flavors.
  • Reduce the heat and add milk to the saucepan.
  • Simmer the chai for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Strain the masala chai into cups using a fine mesh strainer or tea filter.
  • Add sugar or sweetener according to taste preferences and stir until dissolved.
  • Your Masala Chai is ready to be served. Enjoy!
