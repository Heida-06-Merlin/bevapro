Chocolate Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1 cup brewed coffee
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • Whipped cream (optional)
  • Chocolate shavings or cocoa powder for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • Brew a cup of your favorite coffee using your preferred method.
  • In a small saucepan, heat the milk until it's warm but not boiling.
  • Once the milk is warm, add chocolate syrup to it and stir until well combined.
  • Pour the brewed coffee into a mug.
  • Slowly pour the chocolate milk mixture into the coffee while stirring gently.
  • If desired, top with whipped cream and garnish with chocolate shavings or cocoa powder.
  • Your Chocolate Coffee is ready to be served. Enjoy!
