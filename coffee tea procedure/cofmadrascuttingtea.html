Madras Cutting Tea

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1-2 teaspoons loose black tea leaves or 1-2 tea bags
  • Sugar to taste

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add milk to the boiling water and let it simmer for a few seconds.
  • Reduce the heat to low and add black tea leaves or tea bags.
  • Allow the tea to brew for about 2-3 minutes.
  • Strain the tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer.
  • Add sugar according to taste preferences and stir until dissolved.
  • Your Madras Cutting Tea is ready to be served. Enjoy!
