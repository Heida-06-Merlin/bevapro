Chennai Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ground coffee beans
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or sweetened condensed milk (adjust to taste)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add ground coffee beans to the boiling water and allow it to simmer for about 5-10 minutes.
  • Strain the brewed coffee decoction using a filter or sieve into a container.
  • In another saucepan, heat milk until it's warm but not boiling.
  • Now, take a cup and add 1-2 tablespoons of the brewed coffee decoction.
  • Add the warm milk to the cup.
  • Add sugar or sweetened condensed milk according to taste preferences.
  • Stir well until the sugar or condensed milk is dissolved.
  • Your Chennai Coffee is ready to be served. Enjoy!
