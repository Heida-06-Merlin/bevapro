White Tea

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2-3 white tea bags or 2-3 teaspoons loose white tea leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Sliced lemon (optional)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Remove the saucepan from heat and let the water cool for about 1-2 minutes.
  • Add white tea bags or loose white tea leaves to the water.
  • Allow the tea to steep for 4-5 minutes, depending on your desired strength.
  • Strain the white tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer.
  • Add honey or sugar according to taste preferences and stir until dissolved.
  • If desired, add sliced lemon for extra flavor.
  • Your White Tea is ready to be served. Enjoy!
