Ginger Tea

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon freshly grated ginger
  • 1-2 teaspoons loose black tea leaves or tea bags
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • Sliced lemon (optional)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add freshly grated ginger to the boiling water.
  • Reduce the heat and simmer for about 5 minutes to infuse the ginger flavor into the water.
  • Add black tea leaves or tea bags to the saucepan and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Strain the ginger tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer or tea filter.
  • Add honey or sugar according to taste preferences and stir until dissolved.
  • If desired, garnish with sliced lemon.
  • Your Ginger Tea is ready to be served. Enjoy!
