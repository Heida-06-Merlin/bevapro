Lemon Tea

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2-3 black tea bags or 2-3 teaspoons loose black tea leaves
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 2-3 slices of fresh lemon
  • Fresh mint leaves for garnish (optional)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add black tea bags or loose black tea leaves to the boiling water and let it steep for 3-5 minutes, depending on your desired strength.
  • Remove the tea bags or strain the tea leaves using a fine mesh strainer.
  • Add honey or sugar to the brewed tea and stir until dissolved.
  • Squeeze the fresh lemon slices into the tea and stir gently.
  • Divide the lemon tea into cups and garnish with fresh mint leaves if desired.
  • Your Lemon Tea is ready to be served. Enjoy!
