Mylapore Filter Coffee

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup ground coffee beans (preferably a blend of chicory)
  • 1 1/2 cups water
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or jaggery powder (adjust to taste)

Procedure

  • In a coffee filter, add the ground coffee beans.
  • Place the coffee filter over a coffee pot or mug.
  • Boil the water and slowly pour it over the coffee grounds.
  • Allow the coffee to drip through the filter into the pot or mug.
  • In a separate saucepan, heat the milk until it's warm but not boiling.
  • Pour the warm milk into the brewed coffee.
  • Add sugar or jaggery powder according to taste preferences.
  • Stir well until the sugar or jaggery is dissolved.
  • Pour the Mylapore Filter Coffee into cups and serve hot.
