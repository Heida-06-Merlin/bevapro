Cardamom Tea

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2-3 green cardamom pods, lightly crushed
  • 1-2 teaspoons loose black tea leaves or tea bags
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1-2 tablespoons sugar or sweetener (adjust to taste)

Procedure

  • In a saucepan, bring water to a boil.
  • Add lightly crushed green cardamom pods to the boiling water.
  • Reduce the heat and simmer for about 5 minutes to infuse the cardamom flavor into the water.
  • Add black tea leaves or tea bags to the saucepan and simmer for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Now, add milk to the saucepan and simmer for another 2-3 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  • Strain the cardamom tea into cups using a fine mesh strainer or tea filter.
  • Add sugar or sweetener according to taste preferences and stir until dissolved.
  • Your Cardamom Tea is ready to be served. Enjoy!
