Dragonfruit Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe dragonfruit, peeled and diced
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Peel the dragonfruit and dice it into chunks.
  • In a blender, combine the diced dragonfruit and cold water.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any seeds or pulp.
  • Pour the dragonfruit juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Dragonfruit Juice!
