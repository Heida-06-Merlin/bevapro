Lemon Juice

Ingredients

  • 2-3 fresh lemons
  • 4 cups water
  • 1/4 cup sugar or honey (adjust to taste)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Roll the lemons on a flat surface to soften them and make them easier to juice.
  • Cut the lemons in half and juice them using a citrus juicer or by hand.
  • In a pitcher, combine the freshly squeezed lemon juice, water, and sugar or honey. Stir until the sweetener is dissolved.
  • Taste the lemonade and adjust the sweetness by adding more sugar or honey if desired.
  • Refrigerate the lemon juice until chilled or serve immediately over ice cubes.
  • Serve in glasses and garnish with lemon slices if desired.
  • Enjoy your refreshing Lemon Juice!
