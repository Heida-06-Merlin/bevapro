Saathukudi Juice

Ingredients

  • 4-5 saathukudi (citrus fruits) - can use oranges, lemons, or a mix
  • 2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash the saathukudi thoroughly under running water.
  • Roll the saathukudi on a flat surface with your palm to soften them and extract more juice.
  • Cut the saathukudi in half and juice them using a citrus juicer or by hand.
  • Strain the juice to remove any seeds or pulp if desired.
  • In a pitcher, combine the saathukudi juice, cold water, and honey or sugar (if using). Stir well until the sweetener is dissolved.
  • Fill glasses with ice cubes if desired and pour the saathukudi juice over the ice.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Saathukudi Juice!
