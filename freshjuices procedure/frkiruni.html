Musk Melon Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 musk melon (also known as cantaloupe or honeydew), peeled, seeded, and chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash the musk melon thoroughly under running water.
  • Cut the musk melon in half and scoop out the seeds.
  • Peel the musk melon and chop it into chunks.
  • In a blender, add the chopped musk melon and cold water.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any pulp or fibers.
  • Pour the musk melon juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Musk Melon Juice!
