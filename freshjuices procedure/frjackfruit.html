Jackfruit Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe jackfruit, peeled and deseeded
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Prepare the jackfruit by cutting it open, removing the seeds, and separating the flesh from the skin.
  • Place the jackfruit flesh in a blender.
  • Add cold water to the blender.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any fibrous pulp.
  • Pour the jackfruit juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Jackfruit Juice!
