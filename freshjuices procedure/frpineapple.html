Pineapple Juice

Ingredients

  • 1 medium-sized pineapple, peeled, cored, and chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Prepare the pineapple by cutting off the top and bottom, then peeling it and removing the core. Chop the pineapple into chunks.
  • In a blender, add the chopped pineapple and cold water.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any fibrous pulp.
  • Pour the pineapple juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Pineapple Juice!
