Carrot Juice

Ingredients

  • 4-5 large carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 1/2 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • Sugar or honey to taste (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash, peel, and chop the carrots into small pieces.
  • Add the chopped carrots and water to a blender.
  • Blend until smooth and there are no large chunks of carrots remaining.
  • If the juice is too thick, you can add more water to reach your desired consistency.
  • Taste the juice and add sugar or honey if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any pulp or fibers.
  • Pour the carrot juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy!
