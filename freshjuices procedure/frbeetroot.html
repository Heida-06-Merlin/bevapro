Beetroot Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized beetroots, washed and peeled
  • 1 apple, cored and chopped
  • 1-inch piece of ginger, peeled
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 2 cups water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Cut the beetroots into smaller pieces to make blending easier.
  • In a blender, add the chopped beetroots, chopped apple, ginger, lemon juice, honey or sugar (if using), and water.
  • Blend until smooth. If the mixture is too thick, add more water as needed.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any pulp.
  • Pour the juice into glasses over ice cubes, if desired.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing beetroot juice!
