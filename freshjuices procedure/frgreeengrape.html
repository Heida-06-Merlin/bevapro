Green Grape Juice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups green grapes, washed
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash the green grapes thoroughly under running water.
  • In a blender, add the washed green grapes and cold water.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any seeds or pulp.
  • Pour the green grape juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Green Grape Juice!
