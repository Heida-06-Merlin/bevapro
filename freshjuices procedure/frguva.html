Guava Juice

Ingredients

  • 3-4 ripe guavas, washed and chopped
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • 1 cup cold water
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Wash the guavas thoroughly under running water.
  • Cut the guavas into quarters and remove the seeds if desired.
  • In a blender, add the chopped guavas and cold water.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and add honey or sugar if desired for sweetness. Blend again to mix well.
  • Strain the juice using a fine mesh strainer or cheesecloth to remove any seeds or pulp.
  • Pour the guava juice into glasses over ice cubes, if using.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy the refreshing Guava Juice!
