Watermelon Juice

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (optional)
  • 1-2 tablespoons honey or sugar (optional)
  • Ice cubes (optional)

Procedure

  • Place the cubed watermelon in a blender.
  • Add lime juice if desired for a tangy flavor.
  • Add honey or sugar if you want a sweeter juice.
  • Add ice cubes if you want a colder juice.
  • Blend until smooth.
  • Taste the juice and adjust sweetness or tanginess if necessary.
  • Pour the watermelon juice into glasses.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy your refreshing Watermelon Juice!
